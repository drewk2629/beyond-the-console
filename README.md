### <h2><b>Summary</b></h2>
<h3>Beyond The Console is an app that any variety of gamer can use to keep track of their game collection and wishlist. Users can also view other profiles in order to gather new ideas for games they might like to try. Finally, people can comment and review games to help other users find exactly what they are looking for, or something they werenâ€™t!</h3>
